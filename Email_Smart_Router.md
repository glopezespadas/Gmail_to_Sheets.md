# Email Smart Router

## Business Problem

Teams often receive a mix of work-related, personal, and administrative emails. Manually reviewing and categorizing messages consumes time and creates organizational inefficiencies.

## Solution

Built an automated email routing workflow that analyzes incoming Gmail messages, classifies them based on predefined business rules, and logs the results into Google Sheets for tracking and reporting purposes.

## Key Features

* Automated Gmail monitoring
* Rule-based email classification
* Real-time categorization
* Automated Google Sheets logging
* Timestamp tracking for auditability

## Workflow

1. New email received in Gmail
2. Subject line analyzed
3. Routing logic determines category
4. Email classified as:

   * WORK
   * PERSONAL
   * ADMIN
5. Results stored automatically in Google Sheets

## Business Value

* Reduces manual email triage
* Improves organization and visibility
* Creates structured reporting data
* Demonstrates workflow automation capabilities
